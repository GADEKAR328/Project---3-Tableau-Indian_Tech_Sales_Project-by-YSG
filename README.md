# Project- 3 - Tableau-Indian_Tech_Sales_Project-by-YSG
This repository showcases an end-to-end Tableau dashboard project analyzing Indian tech product sales data. The project focuses on identifying sales trends, top-performing products, customer insights, and regional performance across India.

# Project Overview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of tech sales performance:

- 📈 Month-wise Quantity Sold to track seasonal demand patterns
- 🏆 Top Products by Sales (Canon PIXMA, Epson EcoTank, Dell UltraSharp, etc.)
- 🗺️ State-wise Sales Distribution across India using map visualization
- 👥 Top 5 Customers by Sales Contribution
- 📦 Category-wise Comparison (Accessories, Printers, Laptops, Smartphones, etc.)
- 🌍 Top States & Product Preferences for regional insights

# 📊 Key Insights
- Certain products like Canon PIXMA & Epson EcoTank dominate overall sales
- Sales show fluctuations across months, indicating seasonal trends
- Printers and Accessories contribute a significant share of revenue
- A few high-value customers drive major sales volume
- Sales are concentrated in specific states, highlighting regional demand differences
